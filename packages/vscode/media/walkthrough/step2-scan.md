# Run Your First Scan

The **Intent Drift** gauge shows how well your code matches your specs.

1. Open the Shipgate panel (Explorer sidebar)
2. Click **Run Scan** to verify your workspace
3. Check the verdict: **SHIP** (ready), **WARN** (review), or **NO_SHIP** (fix first)

[Run Scan](command:shipgate.runScan)

The scan validates ISL specs, catches ghost references, and surfaces blockers.
